About

AMB. kassy Nwamara

Ambassador Kasiemobi Prisca Nwamara, globally known as Kassy Nwamara in her simplistic and yet sophisticated personality is an icon of humanity and exemplary leadership.

  • Founder of BEEN-THERE FOUNDATION INT'L - a project based NGO committed to empowering women and youths in Africa through skill acquisition projects and donations of work tools and facility for growth and development in skills. The foundation creates platforms and enabling environments to activate aspiration and purposeful thinking in women and youths so that these people can become the best version of themselves.
  • She is the Pioneer of the WIRE Campaign (Women In Real Estate) which creates opportunities for women in real estate.
  • As a passionate media personality, Kassy Nwamara is seen a lot around the corridors of media houses doing what she knows how to do best - inspiring people. As a graduate of Mass Communication she has made her marks on TV and Radio along her career path.
  • In her business brilliance and leadership she is a one time winner of $5,000 from the Tony Elumelu Foundation and today she extends a hand to others in Personal Development and empowerment. Kassy Nwamara has been celebrated with several awards in recognition of her humanitarian works and business leadership. Recently she was recognized with a humanitarian award by NYAGGI - Nigerian Youth Advocacy for Good Governance Initiative.
  • Kassy is a lover of family and holds strong family values. She is happily married to Nelson Nwamara; Author and Founder of HOMLAND Group.
